I am a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with 16 years of experience providing Biblically based mental health therapy in the state of Kentucky. I work with children, adults, couples, and families facing anxiety, depression, substance use, and other life challenges. My goal is to help clients develop healthy coping skills, strengthen emotional well-being, and experience lasting personal growth. I primarily use Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, thoughtfully integrated with faith-based principles.
